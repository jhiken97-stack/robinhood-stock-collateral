  1. Protocol Identity

    Name: Robin Markets Stock Collateral

    Type: Compound v3-style lending market on Robinhood public testnet

    Canonical Market ID: robinhood:46630:0x7C5EE3f540A163947B32D178B3C9A83a65ED6E79

  2. Chain and RPC

    Network: Robinhood Testnet

    Chain ID: 46630

    RPC: https://rpc.testnet.chain.robinhood.com

    Explorer: https://explorer.testnet.chain.robinhood.com

  3. Core Contracts

    Comet: 0x7C5EE3f540A163947B32D178B3C9A83a65ED6E79

  4. Supported User Flows

    Supply collateral: ERC20 approve, then Comet.supply(asset, amount)

    Withdraw collateral: Comet.withdraw(asset, amount)

    Borrow rUSD: Comet.withdraw(baseToken, amount)

    Repay rUSD: ERC20 approve, then Comet.supply(baseToken, amount)

  5. Agent Constraints

    Agents should check market cash before borrow attempts and avoid transactions that would revert.

    Agents should enforce risk checks before borrow and withdraw operations (capacity used and health factor).

    Borrowable asset is only the base token (rUSD), not stock collateral assets.

  6. Safety and Policy

    Agent responses should be informational and operational only.

    Agents must not provide financial, investment, legal, or tax advice.

    Treat this environment as testnet and experimental.
