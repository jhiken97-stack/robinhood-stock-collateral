    Step-by-Step Guide

    Follow these steps to get funded, supply collateral, borrow rUSD, and allocate into savings.

    1. Add Robinhood Testnet to Wallet

      Use one click below, or add manually with Chain ID 46630 and RPC https://rpc.testnet.chain.robinhood.com.

      Network Docs

    2. Claim Testnet ETH + Stock Tokens

      Open Robinhood's faucet and request ETH plus the available test tokens to your connected wallet.

      Open Robinhood Faucet

    3. Supply Stock Tokens as Collateral

      Go to Dashboard tab. In Actions â†’ Collateral, choose token, click Approve, then Supply.

    4. Borrow rUSD

      In Actions â†’ Base, enter amount and click Borrow Base. Keep health factor above liquidation zone.

    5. Verify Position + Risk

      Check Your Position for collateral posted, borrowed amount, health factor, and risk zone.

    6. Allocate Borrowed rUSD into Savings

      Open Savings tab, set allocation amounts, and click Apply Allocations. This is a simulated strategy flow on testnet.

    Known Limitations

    • Savings strategies are simulated and do not route onchain on Robinhood testnet.
    • Points are stored in your browser (localStorage) per wallet, chain, and site URL.
    • Faucet requests may be rate-limited or temporarily unavailable.
